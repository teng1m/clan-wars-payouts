About

Heartfelt thanks to clantools.us for helping WoT clans manage payments and much more for all these years. This tool was created as a successor to their attendance tracker. Currently, this only works on the NA server.

Guide

Log in with your Wargaming account from the home page to get started.

For players

  1. Ask an officer for the day's code.
  2. Enter the code on the home page to mark your attendance.

For officers

  1. Open the admin panel to generate today's code.
  2. Share it with the players who showed up for clan wars.
  3. Review weekly attendance in the admin panel to calculate payout distribution.
